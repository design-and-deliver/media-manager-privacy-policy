MediaManager Privacy Policy

MediaManager is designed to help you save and organize URLs that you share from your browser. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

  • URLs: We collect the URLs that you share with our app.
  • Save Dates: We automatically record the date and time when you save each URL.

How We Use Your Information

  • Storage: We store the URLs and their save dates within your device.
  • Deletion: You can delete saved URLs at any time within the app.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

We do not currently use any third-party services that collect or process your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes.
